A Letter from the Editor




        I
           t is with great pleasure and honor that I write this first letter to
           introduce “Shikorr -Magazine” and me, it is a new beginning

        of an auspicious era of my life. Shikorr -Magazine basically it

        will  be  a  souvenir  –  magazine  for  particular  group  of  Shikorr

        member- community, but it will also publish as a quarterly all the

        year- long for staying connected to our friends and well–wishers.  Shikorr -Magazine, it’s a

        new magazine but a promising one. This magazine is dedicated to bring simple, accessible and

        amusing articles. We are looking forward to spot light you, your experience, and your passion,

        what makes you unique. Writing is an absolute and individualistic act, but the importance of

        sharing your work can’t be ignored. A personal essay or writing can bind you to an aspiring

        audience. If you are a full-time freelance writer or an enthusiast essayist, eager to publish your

        work for the first time, you can finally stop stressing you can submit your work to us, we will

        take care of it for you. And our harmonious and familiar Shikorr ally groups, they are really

        special to us, like our own family; We are eagerly waiting to cover their issues, events and

        their passions, hobbies, passionate writings. we will try our best to rejuvenate Shikorr groups,

        and associations up to their ultimate level. Sometimes people slip unnoticed, under the radar,

        sometimes people, groups and experiences don’t receive the spotlight, the acknowledgement,

        or the media coverage they should get. Our goal is to cover all of them, serve them all in a best

        possible way.  The world is changing noticeably; this is the era of rapid change we are living

        in now, let’s try to change together, try to share our experience, our untold stories together

        with our friends and allies, at the end I am finishing with a William Shakespeare’s quote – “To

        Mingle Friendship Far is Mingling Bloods.”


        Best Regards


        Fouzia Nishat Sultana


        Editor, Shikorr Magazine
